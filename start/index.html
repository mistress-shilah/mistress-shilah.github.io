Protocol Starter: 7 Days

A simple discipline ritual. No drama. Just proof.

  1. Day 1: Clean one area completely. Photograph the result (for your own record).
  2. Day 2: 20 minutes of focused work. No multitasking.
  3. Day 3: Grooming + posture check. Move with intention all day.
  4. Day 4: Fitness: 30 minutes of effort. No excuses.
  5. Day 5: Learn: 30 minutes of reading/study. Summarize 5 sentences.
  6. Day 6: Service mindset: do one helpful task without announcing it.
  7. Day 7: Review: write what you did, what you avoided, and what changes next.
